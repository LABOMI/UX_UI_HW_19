    About Me

    With a background in an exhibition industry, my role in each always involves the project managing, supervising, and working with skateholder. By taking a responsibilites of my role, I was able to solve the urgent problems, work detaily, collaborate with different departments and communicate between two companies. As a UX Desiger, I am able to leverage my background in project managing, solving problems and working with skateholder. I can prioritize client needs and solve the problems in a user-centered way. My process entail creating target users, doing interviews with target users and get an insight from the interview results. Afterthen Identify users’ painpoint and needs. Using heuristic analysis, card sorting, site map and user flow make sure whether it’s being made easier and friendly for people to use. Also, by doing a multiple usertesting and prototype iteration improve the users’ experience.
